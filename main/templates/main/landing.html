How it works
Step 1: Someone (or you) creates a program
A community member makes a Falah account and creates a program, specifing the topic, their expirience, what partner venue it will be hosted at, and more details. While waiting for approval, the creator prepares to teach by planning out lessions and anything they might need to help better empart their knowlege.
Step 2: People sign up for the program
Members of the community sign up or sign their children up to grow their knowlege or skills in a particular way.
Step 3: The program
The creator of the program and attendees meet for the scheduled class(es) and the attendees gain new skills or learn new things.
Step 4: Aftermath
After the program is completed, attendees will rate their expirience. If the ratings are good, the creator of the program is eligible for a certificate of community service.
